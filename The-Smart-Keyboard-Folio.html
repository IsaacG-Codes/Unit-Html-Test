The Smart Keyboard Folio

All About the Smart Keyboard Folio

The Smart Keyboard Folio is a keyboard that attaches magnetically to the back of iPad Pro to provide protection, while the front cover doubles as a keyboard. Simply unfold the keyboard to use it. Or fold it to create a slim, lightweight cover. Released in November 2015 alongside the iPad Pro (1st generation), the Smart Keyboard Folio is Apple's first keyboard cover accessory for iPad. The Smart Keyboard Folio was a great addition to the iPad as the iPad was already a huge success. The Smart Keyboard Folio added the typing capabilities of a regular computer or laptop to the iPad, all while giving it some extra protection. This was a gamechanger for anybody who owned a iPad beforehand; the already highly innovative iPad now could be even better with this new, unique addition to it's immersive working proficiency.The Smart Keyboard is powered by the iPad's Smart Connector, and does not require separate charging or batteries. The Keyboard keys utilize a unique butterfly-switch mechanism, with its keys enveloped by a fabric material. When unfolded, the Smart Keyboard only allows for one viewing angle position; when folded, the Smart Keyboard only protects the front of the iPad. The Smart Keyboard is compatible with iPad Pro models from 2015 to 2017, the iPad Air (3rd generation), and iPad models from 2019 to 2021. At release, it received criticism for its high price tag. The Smart Keyboard Folio is compatible with 11-inch and 12.9-inch iPad Pro models from 2018 and later, and iPad Air models from 2020 and later.
